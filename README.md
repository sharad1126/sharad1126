# Hi, I'm Sharad 👋

> **Senior Risk Strategy Expert** · **PhD Computer Science, UCL** · **Financial Fraud Researcher**

I sit at the intersection of **data analysis**, **financial crime**, and **risk strategy** — turning complex data into actionable decisions that mitigate cybercrime and keep people safer online. My doctoral research at University College London focused on measuring online financial fraud originating from SMS scams, and I continue to publish at the frontier of this space.

## 🧠 About Me

- 🏦 Currently working in **fintech** as a **Senior Risk Strategy Expert**
- 🎓 **PhD in Computer Science** from **University College London (UCL)**
  - Dissertation: *"Measuring Online Financial Fraud Originating from SMS Scams"*
- 🔬 Active researcher in **fraud detection**, **SMS phishing (smishing)**, and **financial crime analytics**
- 📊 Passionate about translating research into real-world risk systems
